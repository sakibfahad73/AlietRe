WHY WE RUN IN THE DARK

As darkness sweeps around the globe in mid-November, 25,000 people worldwide will get up from their armchairs, slip on their red flashing armbands and pull on their running shoes to complete a 5k or 10k.

 

Running separately while connecting digitally across 7 continents, this global movement will light up the night as people hit the road to Run in the Dark for the Mark Pollock Trust and to donate or fundraise for Collaborative Cures, whose mission is to bring people together to cure paralysis in our lifetime.
Run in the Dark 2021 is not currently open for entries. Sign up to our newsletter below to be the first to know when we open entries in Summer 2021.
